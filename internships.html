Internships

If you are looking in ways to expand your interests and stay busy in the summer, here are some summer programms from colleges to help you out!

\

SeaQuest Aquarium Internships: One of our primary goals here at SeaQuest is to create a broader appreciation for the world's ocean ecosystems. We see the task of preparing a new generation of zoologists, marine biologists, and conservationists for their college education and beyond as part of obtaining that goal. Our internships are highly engaging, intensive, and hands-on with the intention of fostering both personal and academic growth. We have a wide variety of internship opportunities available depending on an individual’s interest and experience, including positions in bird-reptile-mammal husbandry, marine husbandry, and guest services. Check out the attached flyers for more information and instructions on how to apply. Click here to sign up !.

MC2 Program at Children’s Hospital: (For current sophomores.) Applications for the Medical Career Collaborative (MC2) program are now open! MC2 is a two year program for high school students who are interested in health, medicine, or research. The program includes an internship component at Denver Health or Children’s Hospital, as well as numerous workshops, field trips, and service learning opportunities. MC2 provides a level of exposure to the medical field that few high school students get to experience. Apply!.

Children’s Hospital Colorado’s Summer Child Health Research Internship – for current Juniors: The Department of Pediatrics offers summer research opportunities for rising high school seniors, college students and first-year medical students. Students will work in the lab with members of the Department of Pediatrics faculty at Children's Hospital Colorado/University of Colorado on the Anschutz Medical Campus. The summer program will introduce students to research opportunities related to child health. The summer internships will include opportunities for students to participate in a lecture research series as well as present their research at the completion of the summer internship. This program is from June 3rd to August 2nd and you will receive a generous stipend. Apply Here!.
